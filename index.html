League Skin Sales

League Skin Sales is an iOS app that allows users to see the latest champion and skin sales. It also helps them to explore every League of Legends champion skins with great ease. Users can save skins they liked to favorites list.

Sales Page

Users can see what's currently on sale. Champions or skins may or may not be on sale depending on the circumstances. Internet connection is required to get latest update.

​Champions Page

List of all the champion are displayed. Click on any of the champion icons to see the list of all skins for that champion. Again, internet connection is required for the first time. Once downloaded, champion icons will be displayed when offline.

​Champion Skin Page

After a certain champion icon is tapped, a list of all the skins for that champion is shown. Champion skins can be added to favorites when the user taps on them. Tapping again will remove the skin from favorites list.

Favorites Page

All the skins that were tapped can be seen here. Press the remove button to remove them from the list.

Device Compatibilty

iPhone 5/5s, iPhone 6/6s, iPhone 6/6s Plus, iPhone 7, iPhone 7 Plus

iPad Air, iPad Air 2, iPad Pro (9.7 inch), iPad Pro (12.9 inch), iPad Retina
